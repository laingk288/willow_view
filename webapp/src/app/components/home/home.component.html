What We Do

Willow View Farm Ltd is a family owned and operated grain farm located in northwest Saskatchewan. Our business focus is to produce high quality cereal, oilseed and pulse grain. We accomplish this through utilizing leading practices and technology.

Regenerative agriculture is a production system Willow View Farm practices and continues to adapt new principles. Focus is placed on minimizing soil disturbance, maintaining soil cover and maximizing crop diversity. Willow View began the no-till practise in 1997. No-till is a practise adopted to minimize soil disturbance. As a result, soil organic matter, nutrient cycling, water infiltration and retention has improved.

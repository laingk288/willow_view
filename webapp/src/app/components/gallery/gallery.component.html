Seeding Season

Seeding operation at Willow View Farm commences in May and completed within four weeks. Two drills are used to direct seed all crops.

Spraying Season

In-crop spraying takes place in June to achieve early weed control on all crops. A Case high clearance sprayer is used to spray crops using a GPS system to apply herbicide to a specific area without any overlap. A fungicide application follows in-crop herbicide in late June and July to protect plants from the onset of disease. In August, cereals and pulses are desiccated with a sprayer application.

Harvest

Harvest commences when crops are sprayed with a desiccant to dry down each plant to allow the crop to be combined using straight-cut headers. The canola is swathed to allow the canola plant to fully mature. The goal is to complete harvest at by the end of September.
